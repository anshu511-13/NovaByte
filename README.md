# NovaByte

Welcome to NovaByte!

NovaByte is a fun Python-based game hub that includes:

🍪 Cookie Clicker – Upgrade your clicks and boost your cookie power

🎲 Russian Roulette – Play a dangerous guessing game against the computer

🔢 Number Quest – Guess the right number!

🎯 Dice Roller – Roll a digital dice with just one click

⌨️ Typing Test – Test your typing skills with a classic sentence

🧠 Mind Reader – Enter a word and watch it magically appear!


This is my beginner project made for fun and learning — I will improve it over time and add more games.
